How long does the battery actually last?

Up to 40 hours with ANC off, and around 28 hours with ANC on, depending on volume level.

Yes. Aether Pulse pairs over standard Bluetooth 5.4 and supports the companion app on both platforms.

Can I use it wired if the battery runs out?

Yes, a 3.5mm cable is included in the box for wired listening at any time, charged or not.

What does the 2 year warranty cover?

It covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship, including the battery and drivers.
